Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning

2025 | 2h 49m | PG-13

In what promises to be the explosive conclusion of the long-running action franchise, Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning once again follows Ethan Hunt (Tom Cruise) as he faces his most personal and dangerous mission yet. With a collapsing world order and enemies from within, the film delivers jaw-dropping stunts, high-octane chases, and emotional depth that closes Hunt's journey with style and suspense.

“Tom Cruise delivers a breathtaking farewell performance in a finale filled with gravity-defying action.” – Variety

“A satisfying, emotional end to one of the greatest action series of our time.” – The Hollywood Reporter

“McQuarrie and Cruise stick the landing in this epic conclusion.” – IGN

Parents Must Know

As with previous entries in the franchise, this film includes intense action sequences, gun violence, chase scenes, and some perilous situations. While there's minimal sexual content and moderate language, the constant tension and realistic violence may not be appropriate for younger viewers. Suitable for older teens and adults.

Content Rating (13+)

  • Sex & Nudity: none
  • Violence & Gore: Moderate
  • Profanity: Mild
  • Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking: Mild
  • Frightening & Intense Scenes: severe

Sex & Nudity

A few scenes of flirting and one kiss. No nudity or sexual situations are shown. Clean for the most part.

Violence & Gore

Several action sequences with hand-to-hand combat, gunfire, car chases, and explosions. Injuries are shown but gore is minimal.

Profanity

Occasional strong language including a few uses of “s**t” and “d**n.” No uses of the F-word detected.

Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking

Some background scenes in bars or parties where alcohol is present, but no excessive use or drug-related content.

Frightening & Intense Scenes

Moments of peril involving train crashes, falling from heights, and narrow escapes. Emotional intensity due to character fate and sacrifice.
