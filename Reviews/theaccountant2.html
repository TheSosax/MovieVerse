The Accountant 2

2025 | 2h 12m | R

The Accountant 2 (2025), directed by Gavin O'Connor, reunites forensic accountant and assassin Christian Wolff (Ben Affleck) with his lethal brother Braxton (Jon Bernthal) to uncover a human trafficking conspiracy. The cast features Cynthia Addai‑Robinson, J.K. Simmons, Daniella Pineda, and Allison Robertson. Premiered at SXSW on March 8, 2025, and theatrically released on April 25, 2025.

Parents Must Know

Rated R by the MPAA for strong violence and language throughout. Common Sense Media recommends it for ages 16+ due to graphic violence, explicit language (“f‑‑‑”, “motherf‑‑‑er”, etc.), implied sex work, alcohol, and smoking. Kids‑In‑Mind describes extreme violence, gunfire, explosions, stabbing, blood, numerous uses of the F‑word, and sexual innuendo.
