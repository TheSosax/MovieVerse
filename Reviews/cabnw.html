Captain America: Brave New World

2025 | 1h 58m | PG-13

Captain America: Brave New World (2025) continues Sam Wilson’s journey as the new Captain America. Directed by Julius Onah, the film blends superhero action with political intrigue as Wilson uncovers a global conspiracy involving President Thaddeus Ross and a dangerous new element—adamantium. With intense action, emotional stakes, and the emergence of Red Hulk, this installment expands the MCU’s Phase Five with thrilling consequences.

Parents Must Know

Captain America: Brave New World is rated PG-13 for intense sequences of violence, action, and strong language. The film includes frequent combat scenes, explosions, and a transformation into Red Hulk that may be frightening for younger viewers. Language includes multiple uses of profanity, including “sh*t,” “bastard,” and “damn.” There is no sexual content or nudity, and substance use is limited to a character’s reliance on experimental medication. Themes of mind control, betrayal, and political corruption make this film best suited for teens aged 13 and up.
