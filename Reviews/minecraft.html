A Minecraft Movie

2025 | 1h 41m | PG

A Minecraft Movie (2025), directed by Jared Hess, is a live-action adventure comedy based on the iconic sandbox video game. The story follows four misfits—Garrett, Henry, Natalie, and Dawn—who are pulled into the Overworld and must master its mechanics to survive. With Jack Black as Steve and Jason Momoa as Garrett, the film blends slapstick humor, game references, and a quest to save both worlds from the villainous piglin queen Malgosha.

“A breezy, chaotic quest that captures Minecraft’s spirit—if not its depth.” – IGN

“Jack Black and Jason Momoa bring pixelated charm to a colorful but scattered adventure.” – Rotten Tomatoes

“A meme-factory with heart, perfect for fans of the game.” – Common Sense Media

Parents Must Know

This PG-rated film includes mild fantasy violence, slapstick humor, and some scary imagery. While there’s no strong language or sexual content, younger children may find certain scenes intense. Recommended for ages 8 and up, especially for fans of the game.

Sex & Nudity

No sexual scenes. A few suggestive jokes from adult characters, including a subplot involving a principal and a Minecraft villager.

Violence & Gore

Fantasy violence includes battles with zombies, piglins, and creepers. Explosions and slapstick combat are frequent but non-graphic.

Profanity

Mild insults like “idiot” and “stupid.” No strong profanity.

Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking

No drug use. One adult character drinks wine in a comedic scene.

Frightening & Intense Scenes

Dark settings, monster attacks, and a few jump scares. The villain Malgosha may be intimidating for younger viewers.
