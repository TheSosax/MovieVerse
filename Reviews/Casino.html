Casino

1995 | 2h 58m | R

Martin Scorsese's Casino plunges viewers into the 1970s Las Vegas, where Robert De Niro and Joe Pesci's characters manage the mob's affairs. The film features a large ensemble cast including Sharon Stone, James Woods, Don Rickles, Alan King, Kevin Pollak, L.Q. Jones, Dick Smothers, Frank Vincent, John I. Bloom, Pasquale Cajano, Melissa Prophet, Bill Allison, and Vinny Vella.

“A violent, dazzling look into the empire of greed and power.” – Janet Maslin, The New York Times

“Casino is Scorsese unleashed — operatic, violent, and unforgettable.” – Empire Magazine

“Sharon Stone delivers the performance of her career.” – Roger Ebert

Parents Must Know

This film contains extremely graphic violence, frequent strong language, drug use, and explicit sexual content. Scenes may be deeply disturbing and intense. Not suitable for children or sensitive viewers. Recommended only for mature audiences.

Content Rating (+18)

  • Sex & Nudity: Moderate
  • Violence & Gore: Severe
  • Profanity: Severe
  • Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking: Severe
  • Frightening & Intense Scenes: Severe

Sex & Nudity

Features strong sexual content including implied oral sex, scenes of sexual thrusting, and brief full and partial nudity (breasts, buttocks).

Violence & Gore

Extremely graphic. Includes repeated stabbings with significant blood, point-blank headshots, brutal beatings, a man's head crushed in a vise, hand broken by a bat, and live burial. Threats and a car explosion are also depicted.

Profanity

Abundant strong language, including over 100 uses of the F-word and extensive use of crude and anatomical language.

Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking

Frequent alcohol consumption, often to excess. Numerous scenes of smoking. Depictions of drug use including snorting cocaine.

Frightening & Intense Scenes

Many scenes feature extreme violence, betrayal, paranoia, and emotional breakdowns. Includes screaming, tension, and disturbing torture/execution moments. The atmosphere is often bleak and chaotic.
