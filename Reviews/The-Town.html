The Town

2010 | 2h 4m | R

The Town is a gripping crime thriller directed by Ben Affleck, following a group of bank robbers in Charlestown, Boston. Doug MacRay (Affleck) leads the crew but finds himself torn when he falls for Claire (Rebecca Hall), a woman unknowingly connected to their latest heist. As the FBI closes in, Doug must choose between loyalty to his criminal past and a chance at redemption. With intense action and strong performances, The Town delivers a thrilling and emotional ride2.

Parents Must Know

Parents must know: The Town is rated R, meaning it contains strong violence, strong language, explicit sexuality, and drug use. The film features intense shootouts, brutal confrontations, and moments of bloodshed. There are scenes of drug use, including cocaine and marijuana, as well as explicit sexual content, including implied intimacy and nudity in a strip club. The language is strong and frequent, making it a mature viewing experience.
