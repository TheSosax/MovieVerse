Ballerina

2025 | 2h 5m | R

Ballerina (2025), directed by Len Wiseman, is a spin-off from the John Wick universe. Ana de Armas stars as Eve Macarro, a ballerina-turned-assassin trained by the Ruska Roma. Set between John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum and Chapter 4, the film follows Eve’s brutal journey of vengeance after her father’s murder. The cast includes Keanu Reeves, Ian McShane, Anjelica Huston, Norman Reedus, and Gabriel Byrne.

Parents Must Know

This film contains intense and stylized violence, frequent strong language, and dark themes including revenge and trauma. While sexual content is minimal, the overall tone is mature and not suitable for viewers under 17. Recommended for adult audiences only.

Sex & Nudity

No nudity or explicit sexual scenes. Some flirtation and close physical contact may occur, but nothing graphic.

Violence & Gore

Stylized but graphic violence: gunfights, knife attacks, flamethrowers, and torture. Bloodshed is frequent and realistic.

Profanity

Strong language throughout, including multiple uses of the F-word and other profanities.

Alcohol, Drugs & Smoking

Characters are seen drinking and smoking in social settings. No depiction of hard drug use.

Frightening & Intense Scenes

Dark atmosphere, emotional breakdowns, and scenes of torture and execution. High tension and psychological intensity throughout.
