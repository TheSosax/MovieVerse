How to Train Your Dragon (2025)

2025 | 2h 5m | PG

How to Train Your Dragon (2025) is a live-action remake of the beloved 2010 animated film. Directed by Dean DeBlois, the movie follows Hiccup, a young Viking who befriends a wounded dragon named Toothless. Together, they challenge the traditions of their dragon-hunting tribe and embark on a journey of courage, friendship, and acceptance. With stunning visuals, heartfelt performances, and a faithful adaptation of the original story, this film delivers a magical cinematic experience for fans old and new.

Parents Must Know

How to Train Your Dragon (2025) is rated PG for sequences of intense action and peril. The film features fantasy violence including dragon battles, sword fights, and explosions. While there is no graphic content, some scenes may be frightening for younger viewers, especially those involving a massive dragon antagonist. Mild language and brief romantic moments are present. The film promotes themes of empathy, bravery, and challenging prejudice, making it suitable for children ages 7 and up.
