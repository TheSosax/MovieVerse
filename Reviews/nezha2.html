Ne Zha 2

2025 | 2h 24m | PG‑13

Ne Zha 2 (2025), directed by Jiaozi, continues the epic Chinese‑mythology saga from the 2019 hit. Ne Zha and Ao Bing—two halves of a celestial pearl—are reincarnated via a magical lotus, but when their village is attacked, they must share one body, struggle with identity, and fight a powerful demonic threat. The film runs 144 minutes and is in Mandarin with subtitles.

Parents Must Know

This film is powerful and violent—PG‑13 territory. It features frequent martial-arts violence, fantasy battle scenes, disturbing imagery, coarse language (“damn,” “bastards,” etc.), mild nudity (characters briefly exposed while using the bathroom), and themes including death, revenge, and identity. Not recommended for children under 12 without guidance.
