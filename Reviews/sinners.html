Sinners

2025 | 2h 17m | R

*Sinners* (2025) is a genre-bending Southern Gothic horror musical directed by Ryan Coogler. Set in 1932 Mississippi, it follows twin brothers returning home to open a juke joint, only to face supernatural terror. With stunning cinematography, a haunting score, and powerful performances—especially Michael B. Jordan in dual roles—the film explores themes of ancestral trauma, racial injustice, and redemption through music and horror.

Parents Must Know

*Sinners* is rated R for strong bloody violence, sexual content, and language. The film includes graphic vampire attacks, ritualistic horror, and intense psychological themes. There are brief sexual scenes and stylized sensuality, but no nudity. Language is strong, with over 30 uses of the F-word. The film’s allegorical depth may resonate with mature teens, but its disturbing imagery and occult symbolism make it unsuitable for younger audiences.
